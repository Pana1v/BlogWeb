The charming Today

Author - Salat Sajiyah
04 January. 6 min read

Every day I wake up with a huge smile, with the hope that today will be the better day that today my fears will wither away completely, today I will not have to put up a fake smile. I have become more and more lonely and a prisoner of my mind, but still have hope that today will come and it will be far better than I imagined.

With this resolve, I continue to push on. And I make little ground everyday. But a little progress is better than nothing isn't it?
